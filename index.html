Main Bahut Sharminda Hoon...

Maine tumhari baat nahi maani, aur wahi meri sabse badi galti thi.

Parso raat jab tum mujhse baat karna chah rahi thi, aur main jabardasti so gaya... wo lamha yaad karke ab mujhe khud par gussa aa raha hai. Tumne haq se mujhse waqt maanga tha, aur maine apni zidd ke aage tumhare jazbaaton ki kadar nahi ki.

Mujhe us waqt samajhna chahiye tha ki tumhari baatein mere liye kitni keemti hain. Maine sirf apni neend dekhi, tumhara intezaar aur tumhara pyaar nahi dekha.

Main janta hoon mera "sorry" kehna kafi nahi hai, kyunki maine tumhara dil dukhaya hai. Par sach mano, tumse door reh kar mujhe ehsas ho gaya hai ki tumhari awaaz sune bina sukoon nahi milta. Meri har zidd tumhare samne choti hai.

Kya ek baar phir se wahi purana wala "hum" ban sakte hain? Main wada karta hoon, ab kabhi tumhari baat ansuni nahi karunga.

Tumhari smile ke bina sab andhera hai...
